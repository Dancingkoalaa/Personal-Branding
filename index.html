About me

Hi! My name is Tim, also known as Koala. I am a student at the Rotterdam University of Applied Sciences. I always had an interest in tech, which grew over the years. My focus is on full-stack development. The reason for this is that I enjoy designing but I also like to work on back-end. Because of my past experience and knowledge from CMGT, I have learned a lot of different languages. Things like React native, typescript, Laravel but also the well known parts like HTML, CSS and JS are in my arsenal of known languages.

I would call myself a very critical and realistic person. Which is in my opinion important when making big projects. Especially when I get asked to give feedback or review someones assignment I will take the time to read the full document and ask critical questions about the products to give them the best help they can recieve

Before I came here, I also did an MBO study for IT. Where I learned the basics of programming. The languages I use the most are HTML, JS and PHP. But I also have experience in different types.

My hope is to end up as a full stack programmer who does both front-end and back-end. I do want to mainly focus on webdevelopment and have a smaller focus on mobile development, although i would be open to the option if i get the chance to work on it
